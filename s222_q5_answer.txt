Section 2.2.2 Question 5 – MNIST classification with SGD (64 neurons, 2 hidden layers)

Test accuracy: 0.9727 (97.27%)

The two-hidden-layer network with 64 neurons per layer and ReLU activations, trained with
SGD (batch size 256, lr=0.1, 10,000 iterations), achieves ~97.3% accuracy on the MNIST
test set. This is a strong result for a simple fully-connected network with no convolutions
or special regularization, demonstrating that feedforward networks with sufficient capacity
can learn meaningful features from raw pixel values.
