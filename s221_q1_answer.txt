Section 2.2.1 Question 1 – Two hidden layers (16 neurons), decision boundary and test accuracy

Test accuracy: 1.00 (100% on the test set)

The network with two hidden layers of 16 ReLU neurons each successfully learns to separate the
three-class spiral dataset that is not linearly separable. The decision boundary is piecewise
linear (due to ReLU activations), creating curved, non-linear regions that closely follow the
spiral structure of the data. The network achieves perfect test accuracy, showing that the
capacity of 16 neurons per layer is sufficient for this 2D problem.
